Global Rule of Law Metrics Dashboard

This dynamic Power BI dashboard analyzes the Rule of Law Index and associated indicators across global nations from 1789 to 2023. Utilizing advanced DAX calculations, it offers dynamic views into historical trends such as judicial independence, access to justice, and legal enforcement predictability. The dashboard facilitates a deep dive into governance evolution at both regional and national levels and cross national comparisons, enabling researchers, policymakers, and security analysts to assess institutional resilience, societal stability, and democratic development trajectories.
